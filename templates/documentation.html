Complete guide to using CardioPredict

Getting Started

What is CardioPredict?

CardioPredict is an AI-powered cardiovascular disease prediction system that uses machine learning to assess your risk of cardiovascular disease based on health parameters. The system analyzes your health data and provides personalized risk assessments with recommendations.

Key Features

  • Real-time cardiovascular disease risk prediction
  • Personalized health recommendations
  • Visual risk assessment with charts
  • Prediction history tracking
  • Download predictions as PDF or CSV
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design
  • Privacy-focused local data storage
  • Comprehensive analytics dashboard

Using the Prediction Tool

Step 1: Navigate to Predict

Click on the "Predict" link in the main navigation menu to access the prediction form.

Step 2: Enter Patient Information (Optional)

Fill in optional patient details:

Field Description Type
Patient Name Full name of the patient Text
Father's Name Father's full name Text
Blood Group ABO blood group (O+, A-, etc.) Dropdown
Phone Numbers Primary and alternative contact Text
Reference Doctor Referring physician's name Text

Step 3: Enter Health Information (Required)

Fill in all required health parameters:

Parameter Description Range Required
Age Patient age in years 1-120 Yes
Gender Male or Female 1 (M), 2 (F) Yes
Height Height in centimeters 100-250 Yes
Weight Weight in kilograms 20-200 Yes
Systolic BP (ap_hi) Systolic blood pressure in mmHg 60-250 Yes
Diastolic BP (ap_lo) Diastolic blood pressure in mmHg 40-150 Yes
Cholesterol Cholesterol level 0=Normal, 1=Above, 2=Well Above, 3=High Yes
Glucose Glucose level 0=Normal, 1=Above, 2=Well Above, 3=High Yes
Smoker Current smoking status 0=No, 1=Yes No
Alcohol Alcohol consumption 0=No, 1=Yes No
Physical Activity Regular physical activity status 0=No, 1=Yes No

Step 4: Get Prediction

Click the "Get Prediction" button. The system will analyze your data and display:

  • Risk Assessment (Low Risk, Moderate Risk, High Risk)
  • Disease Probability Percentage
  • Visual Risk Chart
  • Personalized Health Recommendations
  • Unique Prediction ID

Understanding Your Results

Risk Assessment Levels

Low Risk (0-30%): Your cardiovascular risk is low. Maintain healthy habits with regular exercise and balanced diet.

Moderate Risk (30-60%): You have moderate cardiovascular risk. Consult with a healthcare provider and consider lifestyle modifications.

High Risk (60%+): You have elevated cardiovascular risk. Schedule a comprehensive evaluation with a healthcare professional immediately.

What Do the Percentages Mean?

The disease probability percentage represents the model's confidence level that you may have cardiovascular disease based on the input parameters. This is NOT a medical diagnosis‚Äîconsult a doctor for proper evaluation.

Data Storage & Privacy

Local Storage

CardioPredict stores all prediction data locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • No data is sent to external servers
  • You have complete control over your health information
  • Data persists across browser sessions
  • You can clear data by clearing browser cache

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Issue: Form submission fails
Solution: Check that all required fields are filled and values are within valid ranges.

Issue: Results not displaying
Solution: Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser. Try refreshing the page.

Issue: Predictions not saved
Solution: Check if localStorage is enabled in your browser settings.
