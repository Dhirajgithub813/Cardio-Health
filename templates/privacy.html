Your privacy and data security are important to us

Last Updated: February 6, 2024

1. Introduction

CardioPredict ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") operates the CardioPredict website and application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using CardioPredict, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data: While using CardioPredict, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Patient Name
  • Father's Name
  • Blood Group
  • Phone Number(s)
  • Reference Doctor Name
  • Health Parameters (age, gender, height, weight, blood pressure, etc.)

Usage Data

We may also collect information on how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This may include information such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Device IP addresses

3. Use of Data

CardioPredict uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To process your prediction requests
  • To notify you about changes to our Service
  • To gather analysis or valuable information for service improvement
  • To monitor the usage of our Service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

4. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Local Storage: CardioPredict primarily stores your health data locally in your browser using browser storage technologies. This means your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to do so.

5. Data Retention

Local Data: Data stored locally in your browser will remain until you:

  • Manually delete it through our application
  • Clear your browser's cache and storage
  • Uninstall the application or browser

You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time from the "Analytics & Results" dashboard.

6. Data Sharing

Your personal health data is NOT shared with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • As required by law
  • To protect our legal rights
  • To prevent crimes
  • With your explicit consent

7. Cookies and Tracking

CardioPredict may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. These may include:

  • Session cookies (functional)
  • Preference cookies (remember user settings)
  • Analytics cookies (optional, to improve service)

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third-party sites or services.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 18 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with Personal Data, we immediately delete such information from our servers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
