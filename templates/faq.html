General Questions

What is CardioPredict? ‚ñº
CardioPredict is an AI-powered system that predicts cardiovascular disease risk based on health parameters. It uses machine learning to analyze patient data and provide personalized risk assessments.
Is CardioPredict a medical diagnosis tool? ‚ñº
No. CardioPredict is for educational and informational purposes only. It should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.
Who can use CardioPredict? ‚ñº
CardioPredict can be used by anyone interested in understanding their cardiovascular health risk. However, individuals with existing heart conditions should consult their doctor before using this tool.

Using the System

How do I make a prediction? ‚ñº
1. Go to the "Predict" page
2. Enter your health information (age, gender, height, weight, blood pressure, etc.)
3. Click "Get Prediction"
4. View your personalized risk assessment and recommendations
What information do I need to provide? ‚ñº
Required Information: Age, Gender, Height, Weight, Blood Pressure (Systolic & Diastolic), Cholesterol Level, Glucose Level

Optional Information: Patient Name, Father's Name, Blood Group, Phone Numbers, Reference Doctor Name
How accurate is the prediction? ‚ñº
The accuracy depends on the quality and completeness of the input data. Our model has been trained on large cardiovascular datasets. However, this is an educational tool and should not be relied upon as a substitute for professional medical evaluation.
Can I save my predictions? ‚ñº
Yes! Your predictions are automatically saved locally in your browser. You can view your prediction history in the "Analytics & Results" section. You can also download your predictions as PDF or CSV files.

Data & Privacy

Is my data safe? ‚ñº
Your data is processed securely. Predictions are stored locally in your browser storage and are not transmitted to external servers. Please review our Privacy Policy for detailed information about data handling.
Will my data be shared? ‚ñº
No. Your personal health data is not shared with third parties. It remains under your control and is stored only in your local browser unless you choose to download or share it.
How long is my data retained? ‚ñº
Data stored locally in your browser remains until you clear your browser cache/storage. You can manually delete predictions from the "Analytics & Results" page at any time.

Troubleshooting

What should I do if I get an error? ‚ñº
1. Check that all required fields are filled
2. Ensure your values are within reasonable ranges
3. Refresh the page and try again
4. Clear your browser cache
5. Contact support if the problem persists
Why is my prediction not showing? ‚ñº
Check your browser console (F12) for error messages. Ensure JavaScript is enabled. Try using a different browser. If the issue persists, contact our support team.
Can I use CardioPredict on mobile devices? ‚ñº
Yes! CardioPredict is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface automatically adjusts to fit your screen size.
